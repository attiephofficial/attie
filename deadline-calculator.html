Philippine Legal Deadline Calculator

Compute deadlines under the Rules of Court, with Rule 22 adjustments and Philippine holidays already taken into account.

Rule 22 (Period Computation): The day of the act or event that starts the period is excluded; the date of performance is included. If the last day falls on a Saturday, Sunday, or legal holiday, the time shall not run until the next working day. This calculator applies that rule automatically.

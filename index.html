The Process

How Attie Works

Three steps. No office visit. No retainer required.

1
Tell Us Your Situation

Chat with us directly. You don't need to know the legal terms. Just share what you truthfully know, as best as you can. All information is kept strictly confidential.

2
We Review Your Concern

Your query is processed by AI trained on Philippine law and reviewed by a licensed Filipino lawyer before any response is sent. You get a real answer — not a guess.

3
Receive Your Legal Analysis

Your lawyer-reviewed response is sent to you within 24 to 48 hours via email or chat. Clear, practical, and grounded in Philippine law.

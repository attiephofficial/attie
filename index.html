Questions visitors ask anyway.

Is this a replacement for hiring a lawyer?

No. Attie helps people understand their issue and next steps more affordably. It does not replace formal representation when that is needed.

Will I receive an instant answer?

No. Responses are not sent instantly because each one is researched and reviewed before delivery.

Can I stay anonymous?

Yes. You may use a nickname or alias when submitting your concern, as long as the facts are clear enough for review.

What languages can I use?

You may communicate in English or Filipino.

What if I don't know my legal category?

You can choose Not Sure and describe the problem in plain language.

Does using Attie create an attorney-client relationship?

No. The service is informational and does not by itself create an attorney-client relationship.

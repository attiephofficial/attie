The Problem

"Your legal rights do not disappear because you cannot afford a lawyer. Attie exists to close that gap."

Millions of Filipinos face legal problems every day — disputes with employers, landlords, family members, government agencies. Most of them never get answers. Not because the law does not protect them. But because access to legal information has always been inconvenient, expensive, and slow.

Why Attie Was Made

"You should not have to post on TikTok, call a radio station, or wait months just to get a legal question answered."

Attie was built out of a simple frustration: the platforms that exist for legal help are either inaccessible, too slow, or built for lawyers — not for the people who actually need answers. There was no efficient, convenient, and reliable platform for everyday Filipinos to get legal information. Attie is that platform.

The Smarter Model

"You are not paying less. You are paying right."

A lawyer's consultation fee covers more than just legal knowledge — part of what you pay has nothing to do with your question. It goes to the office rent, the fare, the food, and the time it takes to get to your consultation. With Attie, those costs disappear. Attie is not cheap. It is efficient.

Why It's Different

"Not just faster. Actually reliable."

Speed without accuracy is useless in law. Attie is built differently — every response goes through AI-assisted legal research, reviewed and finalized by a licensed Filipino lawyer before it reaches you. You get the efficiency of technology and the reliability of a real lawyer. Not one or the other. Both.

Why AI

"Using AI is not wrong. Using it incorrectly is."

AI in legal research is not unlike the internet in the early 2000s — people were skeptical, unsure, and cautious. But those who learned to use it correctly gained an enormous advantage. Attie uses AI because it makes legal research faster, more consistent, and more thorough. But a lawyer still reads every response. AI assists. The lawyer decides.

Tools & Services

Know What You Can Do

Most Filipinos do not act on their legal situations — not because they have no rights, but because they do not know what those rights are or where to start. Attie's tools are built to change that. Each one helps you understand your options, identify the right agency, and take the first step with confidence.

⚖️
Philippine Legal Deadline Calculator
"Kailan ba dapat mag-file ng MR? Ilang araw na lang ang natitira?"

Missing a legal deadline can permanently close your case — even if you are right. Compute exact filing deadlines under the Rules of Court, with Philippine holiday exclusions automatically applied.
📄
Legal Form Generator
"Kailangan ko ng demand letter pero hindi ko alam kung paano isulat."

Generate demand letters, Motions for Reconsideration, barangay complaints, and small claims forms — structured correctly and grounded in the applicable rules.
🏛️
Small Claims Guide + Form Generator
"May utang sa akin. Pwede ko bang i-sue nang hindi nagbabayad ng abogado?"

The small claims court was designed so ordinary Filipinos can collect what they are owed without a lawyer. Know what to file, the fees, and what to expect on hearing day.
🏠
Landlord-Tenant Rights Checker
"Pinalis ako ng may-ari nang wala mang abiso. Legal ba 'yun?"

Illegal ejectment, utility cutoffs, lockouts, and withheld deposits are among the most common housing violations. Know whether what happened crosses a legal line — and what your next move is.
💼
Labor & Employment
"Basta nalang ako tinanggal sa trabaho"

Illegal dismissal, unpaid wages, NLRC filing, employment contracts.
📞
Debt & Collection Harassment Detector
"Tinatawagan ang mga contacts ko at sinasabihan na may utang ako."

Having a debt does not make harassment legal. Collection agents who threaten, shame, or contact your family are violating the law. Identify the violations and which agency to report them to.
🏥
HMO / Insurance Claims Checker
"Tinanggihan ng HMO ang claim ko. Tapos na ba ang laban?"

A denial from your HMO or insurance company is not the final word. The Insurance Commission has jurisdiction over these disputes — know what steps to take before accepting the denial.
🛍
Consumer Rights Complaint Generator
"Binili ko ang produkto pero sira. Hindi nila tinatanggap para i-refund."

The Consumer Act guarantees your right to a refund, replacement, or repair. Know which agency — DTI, FDA, or BSP — handles your complaint and generate a structured letter.
✈️
OFW Contract Red Flag Checker
"Ang layo na. Nagbabago na ang kontrata dito sa ibang bansa."

Know what POEA protections must be in your contract before you sign — and what your rights are if those terms are violated abroad.
🤝
Barangay Complaint Guide
"Gusto ko mag-reklamo sa kapitbahay ko. Saan ba dapat pumunta?"

Most disputes between neighbors must go through barangay conciliation first. Know the process, what to bring, and what to do if conciliation fails.
⚡
Cyberlibel Checker
"May nag-post ng kasinungalingan tungkol sa akin online."

Being defamed online is a criminal offense under RA 10175 — but not every post qualifies. Know whether what happened meets the legal elements and how to file with the NBI Cybercrime Division.
🏚
HSAC / Condo & HOA Dispute Guide
"Ang HOA namin ay nangongolekta ng dues pero walang ginagawa."

Condo corporations and homeowners associations are not above the law. The HSAC has jurisdiction — most unit owners never know they can file a complaint there.
🎓
School & University Dispute Guide
"Pina-alis ang anak ko sa eskwelahan nang walang malinaw na dahilan."

Schools cannot expel or suspend students arbitrarily. CHED has a complaints process most parents never use. Know what protection exists before it is too late.
📱
Online Lending App Abuse Checker
"Tinatawagan ng lending app ang lahat ng contacts ko."

Accessing your contacts, posting your photos, and shaming you publicly are illegal — regardless of whether you owe money. Know which agency regulates your lending app.
📦
E-Commerce Scam Complaint Generator
"Na-scam ako sa Shopee / Lazada / Facebook Marketplace."

Know whether to escalate to DTI, NBI Cybercrime, or PNP — and generate a structured complaint letter addressed to the right agency in one step.
🔐
Data Privacy Violation Checker
"Ginagamit ng employer ko ang aking impormasyon nang wala akong pahintulot."

The Data Privacy Act gives every Filipino the right to control their personal information. Know how to file a complaint with the National Privacy Commission.
⚠️
Online Investment Scam Reporter
"Nag-invest ako sa online na negosyo. Ngayon, hindi na makuha ang pera."

Know whether what happened is a Ponzi, pyramid, or unregistered securities scheme — and how to file with the SEC before the trail goes cold.
👤
Fake Account / Identity Theft Guide
"May fake account na gumagamit ng pangalan at litrato ko."

Know how to preserve evidence, report to PNP Cybercrime and NBI, and submit platform takedown requests — in the right order, before evidence is deleted.
🖼️
Unauthorized Photo / Content Use Checker
"Ginamit ng ibang tao ang aking litrato nang walang pahintulot."

Your photos and creative work are protected by law. Know your remedies under the IP Code and the Data Privacy Act when someone uses them without permission.
💻
Remote Work & Freelancer Dispute Guide
"Hindi binayaran ng client ko. Saan ba ako dapat mag-reklamo?"

Know whether NLRC or regular courts have jurisdiction over your dispute — and what documents to gather before you file.
💳
Buy Now Pay Later / Fintech Dispute Checker
"May na-charge sa GCash / BNPL ko na hindi ko ginawa."

Unauthorized charges, hidden fees, and illegal interest rates on fintech platforms are a growing problem. The BSP Consumer Protection framework covers you — know how to use it.
📲
Digital Estate & Account Access Guide
"Namatay ang kamag-anak ko. Paano makukuha ang pera sa GCash niya?"

Know what documents courts and platforms require before heirs can access GCash, bank apps, and crypto wallets.
🏗️
Corporation Registration Checklist
"Gusto kong mag-register ng company sa SEC pero hindi ko alam kung saan magsisimula."

Get a complete SEC registration checklist for OPC, stock, or non-stock corporations — so you arrive prepared, not turned away for a missing document.
📋
Annual GIS Filing Guide
"Nakalimutan naming mag-file ng GIS. Magkano na ang multa?"

The GIS is the most commonly missed SEC deadline — penalties compound fast. Know your exact due date, what to include, and how to file electronically.
📅
BIR Deadline Calendar Generator
"Lagi akong na-penalty sa BIR dahil nalilimutan ko ang mga deadline."

Generate a personalized BIR compliance calendar based on your registered tax types — monthly, quarterly, and annual filings so no deadline catches you off guard.
🔍
BIR Form Finder
"Anong BIR form ang gagamitin ko para dito?"

Describe what you need to file in plain language — get the exact BIR form number, where to file it, and when it is due. No guessing.
🏪
DTI Business Registration Guide
"Gusto kong mag-negosyo. Kailangan ko bang mag-register sa DTI?"

Get the complete guide: documents, fees, and what happens if your DTI registration lapses before renewal.
👔
DOLE Employer Compliance Checklist
"May inspection ang DOLE sa aming negosyo. Handa ba kami?"

Know what obligations apply to your business size and industry — before the DOLE inspector arrives at your door.
🛡️
SSS / PhilHealth / Pag-IBIG Employer Guide
"Nagtatanong ang mga empleyado ko kung bakit hindi pa naka-remit ang contributions nila."

Get the correct remittance schedule, contribution rates, and electronic filing instructions for all three agencies — before late remittances turn into penalties.
🌐
PEZA / BOI Incentives Eligibility Checker
"May paraan ba para makatipid sa taxes ang aming negosyo nang legal?"

PEZA and BOI incentives under the CREATE Act are among the most underutilized benefits for qualifying Philippine businesses. Find out if yours qualifies.
